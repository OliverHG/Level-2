About Me

As an offshore racer and delivery sailor, I have competed in races all around the world, including the Whitbread Round the World Race and 1988 America's Cup. I have been able to use this experience to design and engineer components and solutions for racing and cruising yachts all over the globe. From AC72 catamarans to 12 metres, Volvo race yachts to +30m Superyachts.

I have been involved with 5 America's Cup campaigns since I began, as a bowman, in 1987.

Yacht steering systems, Superyacht transom doors and rotating anchor arms are my speciality.

The Designer himself has had vast experience in the industry as both a boat owner, designer and even as a professional sailor.

The Company works closely with other designers and boat builders to try and deliver the best possible outcome for our clients.

Bob Graham design has worked on a range of projects across the years from designer small components of boats to the whole vessel.

We can design a range of different components for your needs, But Yacht steering systems, Superyacht transom doors and rotating anchor arms are my speciality.
