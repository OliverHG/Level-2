Bob Graham Design is a Boat Design Company being run out of Auckland, New Zealand.

The Designer himself has had vast experience in the industry as both a boat owner, designer and even as a professional sailor.

The Company works closely with other designers and boat builders to try and deliver the best possible outcome for our clients.

Bob Graham design has worked on a range of projects across the years from designer small components of boats to the whole vessel.

We can design a range of different components for your needs, But Yacht steering systems, Superyacht transom doors and rotating anchor arms are my speciality.

Bob Graham Design has designed components for both Luxury Yachts, Individual Boats to the Fastest vessels in the sporting side of boating.
